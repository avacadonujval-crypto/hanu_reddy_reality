What We Offer

Comprehensive Real Estate
Services

Four decades of expertise in residential, commercial, and joint venture real estate across India and the USA.

01

Residential Sales & Purchase

Our residential team guides buyers and sellers through every aspect of property transactions, ensuring optimal outcomes through deep market knowledge and transparent advisory.

  • Identify suitable properties for buyers and prospective buyers for sellers
  • Negotiate sale/purchase agreements on behalf of clients
  • Advise on title transfers, registration, and documentation
  • Post-sale support and follow-through
Brokerage: 2% within city limits · 3% outside city limits (from each party)
02

Commercial Leasing & Sales

We connect businesses with ideal commercial properties and match landlords with quality tenants through expert market intelligence and meticulous lease negotiation.

  • Identify suitable properties for tenants and suitable tenants for lessors
  • Negotiate lease terms and rental agreements
  • Market advisory and comparative analysis
  • Lease renewal and exit management
Rental brokerage: 1 month rent from each party · Renewal: 15 days commission from each
03

Joint Venture Development

We facilitate strategic partnerships between land owners and reputable builders, bringing together the right parties to unlock the full potential of every parcel of land.

  • Identify suitable developers for land owners and vice versa
  • Negotiate joint venture terms and revenue sharing arrangements
  • Development advisory and project oversight support
  • Legal documentation and due diligence coordination
Fee: 2% from developer and owner on total land value
04

Industrial & Agricultural Land

Specialist advisory for industrial plots, warehousing sites, and agricultural land transactions outside city limits, with deep knowledge of regulatory frameworks and valuation norms.

  • Site identification and due diligence for industrial buyers
  • Agricultural land transaction compliance advisory
  • Conversion and land use change guidance
  • Market valuation and negotiation support
4% on sale consideration from each party (outside city limits)

GST Notice: GST @ 18% will be charged extra on all commissions and fees. *Conditions Apply. Please contact your nearest Hanu Reddy Realty office for detailed terms.

Our Story

A Legacy Built
on Trust

Hanu Reddy Realty was founded in 1984 in California, USA by Hanu Reddy, a Stanford-educated engineer with a vision to transform how families find their homes. Over four decades, that vision has grown into one of South India's most respected real estate firms.

Holding an MS in Construction Management from Stanford University and a BE in Civil Engineering, Hanu Reddy brings unparalleled technical expertise and market insight to every transaction. He has personally assisted over 5,000 families in finding their dream homes and overseen more than $1 billion in successful sales.

The firm's ethos, "Performance... Not Promises," is not a tagline — it is a covenant renewed with every client, every day, across all 14 offices.

Our Journey

Four Decades of Excellence

1984
Founded in California, USA by Hanu Reddy. The firm's first transactions were NRI-focused real estate advisory.
1995
First international branch opened in Chennai, India — marking the beginning of a pan-India footprint.
2000s
Expansion to Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Pune as South India's real estate market surged. Multiple landmark transactions established the firm's reputation.
2010s
Offices launched in Coimbatore, Mysuru, and Visakhapatnam, bringing trusted advisory to Tier-2 cities. SPP Gardens and Hanu Reddy Residences ventures established.
2026
14 offices across India and the USA. Serving 5,000+ families and growing, with $1B+ in cumulative sales. Global clientele spanning NRIs, domestic investors, and institutional partners.
